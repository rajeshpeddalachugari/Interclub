All work & no play doesn't just make Jill and Jack dull, it kills the potential of disovery, mastery, openness to embrace the change and it hinders innovation and invention!!!

This ancient saying still holds true, even in today's work environment where long working hours take a toll on ones psyche. There is always a need to provide them with a source of recreation and to strike a balance between Professional and Personal life!!!

In our constant endeavor to make a mark for ourselves, we have encouraged and involved people beyond their skills at work. Interclub, an employee association within the organization elected by the employees of HCCI, consists of vibrant and energetic enthusiasts who hold responsibility for employees recreation and entertainment.

Creating an atmosphere of fun is one way to improve the work environment. Studies have proved that people who are cheerful and fun loving are more successful than that of others. Working with the jolly mind set and cheerful attitude acts as a catalyst to enhance performance. Active participation not only boosts ones confidence, but also makes one an ideal candidate.

Our sole aim at Interclub is to create a work atmosphere that is conducive in nurturing hidden talents and creating the best and energetic workforce. Interclub actively coordinates and organizes a gamut of events ranging from inhouse events to outdoor corporate gatherings like Company Day and Family Picnic. Active participation from employees is witnessed in all the events organised by Interclub. We at interclub always strive to have more employee participation and showcase their talents. It is Interclub which makes HCCI a dynamic workplace.

HCCI is a plural society. We can decipher HCCI as a repository of multiplicity of cultures as we have our colleagues hailing from different regions of India. Interclub organizes and celebrates Unity in diversity. Cultural activites at HCCI organized by Interclub links employees from different geographical locations and reinforces the significance of Culture and Traditions.

Events organized by Cultural Team have always brought in a lot of participation and showcased the hidden talents of the employees. Our sole idea is to find a place where we would be able to map the mutual relationship between the techies and culture, and examine their reciprocity.

Cultural Committee organizes one event per month under "Event of the Month" program which covers a wide variety of activities both on and off campus. Cultural committee aims to bring out talents from the employees in all possible forms be it music, dance, theatre, literary skills, sketching or other fine artstyles. In order to showcase employee talents variety of events are organized throughout the year.

Cultural committee also organizes various festivals like Dussera, Diwali, Onam, Ramzan, Christmas along with few regional festivals like Pongal and Ugadi. This helps to sensitize all towards the wide spectrum of beliefs which have harmoniously coexisted in India and spreading the message of peace and brotherhood which have transpired since ages through these festivals.

It's a famous quote "A sound body has a sound mind" depcits that the health of the body is essential for success in life. Sports are commonly defined as an organized, competitive and skillful physical activity requiring commitment and passion to play.

In Games & Sports the physical and mental capabilities are the sole or primary determiner of the outcome. If one is bodily fit, one feels capable of hard endeavor with greater confidence. Sports also teach teamwork giving oneself a sense of pride and accomplishment. Learning to lose is part of learning to win.

We at HCCI encourage and engage employees in a plethora of games and sports activities. The state of art infrastructure and facilities in the organization provide a fillip to the enthusiasts. Well-equipped gymnasium neighboured with table tennis, Carroms, Foosball and Snookers provide a good deal of excitement and entertainment at HCCI.

Interclub encourages various games & sports events such as Badminton, Cycling, Carroms, Chess, Cricket, Women Cricket, Football, foosball, Swimming, TableTennis, Throwball, Volleyball. Internal tourneys are organized for different games and sports by sports committee. Victors of HCCI Internal tournaments will be sponsored by Interclub to represent HCCI at corporate level.

If you would like to subscribe for regular sports updates, send an email to Sports Committee

There is always a question which haunts many of us about what we are doing for our soceity, apart from leading a monotonous life cycle of coming to office and spending the pay check that we get by end of the month. You may say that you are paying hefty amounts to government in the form of taxes which in return takes care of people who are in need. If that is your answer, it is definitely not the right one!

Corporate Social Responsibility is best achieved when societal responsibility is integral part of our life. Interclub does different CSR activities to lend a helping hand for the poeple who are in need. The main objective of CSR activities is to lighten up the spirits in the people by helping them in all possible ways.

Our employees have participated and contributed to the cause of social welfare, education and skills development, women empowerment among others. Please find our recent activities which had a great effective response from employees. Our CSR volunteers play a great role in doing their bit in CSR activites.If you are interested to be a part of our CSR activities, Please send an email to D-IN-CSR VOLUNTEERS.

Fund raising events for Flood Victims (Uttarakhand & AndhraPradesh) Blood Donation Drives, School Kit Drives, Go Green Activites, Blanket Distribution Drives, Organ Donation Camps, Materialistic Donations for the needy
