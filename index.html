All work & no play doesn't just make Jill and Jack dull, it kills the potential of discovery, mastery, openness to embrace the change, innovation and invention!!!

This ancient saying still holds true, even in today's work environment where long working hours take a toll on ones psyche. There is always a need to provide them with a source of recreation to strike a balance between Professional and Personal life!!!

In our constant endeavor to make a mark for ourselves, we have encouraged and involved people beyond their skills at work. Interclub, an employee association within the organization elected by the employees of HCCI, consists of vibrant and energetic enthusiasts who hold responsibility for employees recreation and entertainment.

Creating an atmosphere of fun is one way to improve the work environment. Studies have proved that people who are cheerful and fun loving are more successful than that of others. Working with the jolly mind set and cheerful attitude acts as a catalyst to enhance performance. Active participation not only boosts ones confidence, but also makes one an ideal candidate.

Our sole aim at Interclub is to create a work atmosphere that is conducive in nurturing hidden talents and creating the best and energetic workforce. Interclub actively coordinates and organizes a gamut of events ranging from inhouse events to outdoor corporate gatherings like Company Day and Family Picnic. Active participation from employees is witnessed in all the events organised by Interclub. We at interclub always strive to have more employee participation and showcase their talents. It is Interclub which makes HCCI a dynamic workplace.

HCCI is a plural society. We can decipher HCCI as a repository of multiplicity of cultures as we have our colleagues hailing from different regions of India. Interclub organizes and celebrates Unity in diversity. Cultural activites at HCCI organized by Interclub links employees from different geographical locations and reinforces the significance of Culture and Traditions.

Events organized by Cultural Team have always brought in a lot of employee participation and showcased their hidden talents. Our idea is to find a place where we would be able to map the mutual relationship between the techies and culture, and examine their reciprocity.

Cultural Committee organizes one event per month under "Event of the Month" concept which covers a wide variety of activities both on and off campus. Cultural committee aims to extract talents from the employees in all possible forms be it music, dance, theatre, literary skills, sketching or other fine art styles. In order to showcase employee talents variety of events are organized throughout the year.

Cultural committee also organizes various festivals like Dussera, Diwali, Onam, Ramzan, Christmas along with few regional festivals like Pongal and Ugadi. This helps to sensitize all towards the wide spectrum of beliefs which have harmoniously coexisted in India and spreading the message of peace and brotherhood which have transpired since ages through these festivals.
